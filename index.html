OutZone Tribute

Out Zone is a Shoot'em up game based in vertical scrolling levels. You control a cyborg and fight an alien race through seven different levels with increased difficulty in each one. at the end of each level you must defeat a boss to continiue to the next one.

Out Zone is a never-ending loop game. This means you can play from the first to the last level and if you finish the last one, the game will continue with the first level again (with an increased difficulty).

In this tribute, we will reproduce the stage 4 of the game.
